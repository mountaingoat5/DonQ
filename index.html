notes

Some books, such as "The Ten Books of Fortunes of Love" were unable to be located, so instead are linked to a book containg material structures around the content. Other books (which happen to be quite small or short in reality) are linked to an Anthology, or single resource containg all of these texts. These books are generally the Pastural Romances, or poetry.
